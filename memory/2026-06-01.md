### 1. Fixed Hero Background Zoom

- **Problem:** The background image `/hero-pc-bg.webp` in the hero section was extremely zoomed in.
- **Root Cause:** The class name used was `bg-fill`, which is not a standard Tailwind or CSS background-size class. Because it was ignored, the browser defaulted to `background-size: auto;`, making the large 1920x1071 image render at its full native resolution, which clipped off the left and right arches and zoomed in on the center of the image.
- **Solution:** Replaced `bg-fill` with `bg-cover` in [Hero.tsx](file:///c:/Users/karim/Desktop/islamteban/components/sections/Hero.tsx#L35) to correctly scale the background image to cover the viewport while maintaining its aspect ratio.
