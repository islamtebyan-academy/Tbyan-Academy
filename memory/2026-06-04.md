### 1. Updated Hero Background Sizing to Prevent Cropping and Zooming
- **Problem:** The background image `/hero-pc-bg.webp` in the hero section was using `bg-contain`, which left empty space/borders on certain viewports, or previously used `bg-cover`, which caused zooming and cropping of critical details.
- **Root Cause:** In modern desktop viewports of various aspect ratios, neither `bg-cover` nor `bg-contain` would fully stretch the image to the edges without either cropping the edges or leaving blank borders.
- **Solution:** 
  - Updated [Hero.tsx](file:///c:/Users/karim/Desktop/islamteban/components/sections/Hero.tsx) background styling to use `bg-cover md:bg-[length:100%_100%]`.
  - On mobile, `bg-cover` is retained to keep the portrait image presentation centered and attractive without squishing.
  - On desktop/tablet (from `md` breakpoint and up), `md:bg-[length:100%_100%]` (equivalent to `background-size: 100% 100%`) is applied. This stretches the image to fit the container's exact width and height without any cropping, zooming, or blank margins.
